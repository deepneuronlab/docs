## 1. AI Answers and Proposals

The checklist provides a structured overview based on the **assignment criteria** and displays all questions of the **selected checklist** in chronological order.

Based on the **chosen checklist** and the **assignment criteria**, the AI proposes answers using precise logic. Related questions are identified and answered consistently and coherently. AI-answered questions are indicated by a **blue border** next to the checklist and **blue highlighted answer buttons**. The AI becomes better at securely answering checklist questions with every follow-up assignment created in the App.

For most questions, AI will suggest answers based on the pre-selected assignment criteria. Questions with AI proposals are highlighted **yellow**. A proposal can be **accepted** by clicking the **yellow checkmark**, or **rejected** by clicking the **red cross**.

The **auditor** or **reviewer** can manually edit any question and **adjust AI-generated answers as needed**.

## 2. Answer Question

Questions must be answered by choosing the options **Yes**, **No**, or **n.a.** (not applicable). Once answered, the question will be marked as checked. This will be visualised by a **blue border** on the answer's left side.

**Answer options:**

- **Yes** = issue is material and disclosures are correct, or issue is not material but disclosures have been provided and are correct.
- **No** = issue is material and disclosures are incorrect or insufficient, or issue is not material but disclosures have been provided and are incorrect.
- **n.a.** = issue is not material and therefore the question is not applicable.

## 3. Dynamic Questions

AI recognizes and autofills answers for certain interlinked questions. For example, answering a question with **n.a.** triggers the AI to automatically answer all relevant questions adequately.

A window called **"Automated response"** will appear in the lower right corner of the screen, informing the auditor about interlinked questions. Clicking on the respective question number will lead to the auto-answered question in the Checklist section.

## 4. Information

The general information right below the question in the Checklist section provides **additional legal details** about the question and the related sources (applicable for some questions), as well as information about latest edits regarding time and editor.

The **"A" symbol** in the information box indicates that the answer to this question might be found in a supporting document.

**Online information sources** are provided as links, allowing you to easily view the original content.
