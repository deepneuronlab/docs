## Overview

The account mapping interface is split into two panels:

- **Left panel — "Open"**: Shows all unmapped accounts with a blue count badge. Each account card displays AI proposals you can accept or reject.
- **Right panel — "Mapped"**: Shows all successfully mapped accounts in a hierarchical taxonomy tree with a green count badge.

## Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Assignment Initialization">
    When you open an account mapping assignment, the system loads all accounts from the trial balance. The AI automatically analyzes accounts and generates mapping proposals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Processing">
    A loading dialog shows progress through four stages:
    1. "Accounts get analyzed"
    2. "Mapping accounts"
    3. "Mapping results get validated"
    4. "Results get prepared"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Proposals">
    After AI processing completes, accounts with confident matches are automatically mapped. Remaining accounts show proposals you can accept, reject, or override.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map Priority Accounts">
    Priority accounts are highlighted and should be mapped first. Use the **"Jump to priority account"** button to navigate directly to the next unmapped priority account. A progress bar shows how many priorities have been mapped.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Target Values (optional)">
    For better AI accuracy, import **target values** — expected totals per taxonomy position. The AI uses these to validate and improve its proposals via mathematical matching.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-run AI (optional)">
    After mapping priority accounts or adding target values, click **"AI Mapping"** to re-run the AI with the updated information. This often resolves remaining unmapped accounts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
After AI processing, a success dialog shows how many accounts were automatically mapped and how many have suggestions. Follow the hints to map priority accounts and add target values for the best results.
</Tip>
