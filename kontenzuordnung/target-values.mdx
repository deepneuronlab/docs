## What Are Target Values?

Target values are the expected totals per taxonomy position. When provided, the AI uses mathematical matching to validate its proposals — significantly improving accuracy.

<Note>
Target values enable the AI to automatically assign accounts to their corresponding taxonomy positions. The target value comparison is carried out with a precision of two decimal places.
</Note>

## Import Target Values

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the dialog">
    Click **"Add target values"** at the bottom of the unmapped accounts panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter values">
    The dialog shows a table with all taxonomy positions. For each position, enter the expected **current year** and **previous year** values.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Values are saved automatically as you type. Press **Enter** to move to the next cell.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Calculated Values

When a taxonomy position has child positions with target values, the parent value is automatically calculated from the sum of its children. These are marked with a **"Calculated"** indicator.

## Re-run AI After Adding Targets

After entering target values, click the **"AI Mapping"** button in the unmapped accounts panel to re-run the AI with the new information. The AI will use mathematical matching to validate its proposals against your target values.

<Tip>
Adding target values often resolves the majority of remaining unmapped accounts in a single AI re-run.
</Tip>

## Delete Target Values

To remove target values:
- Click **"Delete Target Values"** to clear all entered values
- Click **"Delete File"** to remove an uploaded target values file
- A confirmation dialog will appear before deletion
