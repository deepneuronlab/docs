## Clients Overview

The Clients Overview displays all clients in your organization. You can search, sort, and filter clients.

## Edit a Client

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Client">
    Click on the desired client in the overview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Details">
    Change the **client name** or **client number** as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Confirm your changes by clicking **"Save"**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Delete a Client

A client can be deleted if you have the appropriate permissions. Deleting a client will also remove all associated assignments.

<Warning>
Deleting a client cannot be undone. Make sure all relevant data has been exported before deleting a client.
</Warning>
