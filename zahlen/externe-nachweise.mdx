## 1. AI-validated External Evidence

AI automatically checks whether external evidence in the supporting documents matches the corresponding figures in the main document.

When such a match is found, the **"External" button** (paperclip icon) in the numbers section will be highlighted **green**. This indicates that the AI has found evidence to validate a figure.

The AI automatically validates and documents the consistency as consistent or inconsistent. By clicking on the **"View"** next to the evidence, the evidence will be scrolled into view and highlighted in the document.

## 2. AI-proposed External Evidence

If the AI requires the auditor's judgment, it adds the found evidence as AI Proposal. If a figure has only AI proposal the **"External"** button appears in **yellow.**

To confirm an AI Proposal, click on the **"Accept"** button (check icon). To reject an AI Proposal, click on the **"Delete"** button (bin icon).

<Note>
AI Proposals will not be part of the export until they have been accepted.
</Note>

## 3. Adding External Evidence to Figures

There are two ways to add evidence to a figure:

### 1. Add evidence directly from the supporting document

A figure can be linked directly from a supporting document to an open figure in the numbers section. To do so, the respective figure must be opened in the **numbers section**. Then, select the evidence in the supporting document using the **cursor**. A menu will automatically appear, which allows adding selected text or number by clicking **"Use as Evidence."**

### 2. Add evidence via the numbers section

Alternatively, evidence can be added by clicking **"+ Add Evidence"** within the **Evidence tab** of the opened figure. This will automatically open the **selection mode**, highlighting the figure to be checked. The evidence can then be marked in the supporting document. The selected evidence will automatically be linked to the corresponding figure.

## 4. View Evidence in the Document

By clicking on the **"View"** next to the evidence, the system will automatically scroll to the correct page and highlight the evidence in the document.

## 5. See the Connected Figures of an Evidence

By clicking on a highlighted evidence in the document, a menu with all connected figures opens. Click on a figure in the menu to open it in the numbers section.

## 6. Delete External Evidence

To delete an evidence, open the figure in the numbers section and click on the **"External"** tab. Connected evidence can be deleted by clicking the **"Delete"** (bin icon).

## 7. Automatic Re-Validation after Uploading New Document Version

Uploading a new version of a document automatically checks all linked evidences for changes.

If an evidence has changed in a new document version, the status of the figure will be set back to **"AI-Proposal"** (yellow), and the connected figures get **Flagged by AI,** indicating that the changes need to be re-evaluated. The consistency state of the evidence gets updated automatically.

If an evidence could not be found in the new document version, the evidence will be evaluated as **"lost,"** and the connected figures will automatically be set to **"Flagged by AI".** This indicates that the evidence might need to be reconnected. To reconnect the lost evidence, delete the incorrect evidence and add an updated evidence.
