## Review Numbers

In **review mode**, all features available in **prepare mode** remain fully accessible. A reviewer has permission to check and review all figures in **ai/numbers**.

Figures can be marked as reviewed using the **double-check icon** (✓✓), supporting a systematic completion of the review process.

Multiple numbers or entire pages can be reviewed simultaneously by selecting the **checkboxes** to the left of each figure. This action opens a bar at the bottom of the screen, enabling tasks such as adding remarks, flagging numbers, accepting or rejecting figures, or deleting numbers.

<Warning>
**Note:** Once a figure is marked as reviewed, it is no longer editable in this status. Any changes require the removal of the review mark before continuing with the audit.
</Warning>
