## Automated Figure Extraction with AI

AI analyzes the entire main document, detects all relevant figures, and lists them in the **numbers section** on the left side of the screen.

Each figure is organized by the page number of the document and is also **highlighted within the document** on the right side of the screen.

## Add Figures Manually

It is possible to manually add figures from the main document by drawing a box around the desired figure with the cursor.

From the appearing menu box, select **"Add to audit."**

The figure will then be added to the corresponding page in the numbers section and can be audited.

It is also possible to add entire tables by drawing a box around them. All figures in the table will be added separately to the audit.

<Note>
**Note:** Adding figures from supporting documents is not possible.
</Note>

## Audit Figures

To ensure a complete and consistent audit, each figure can be verified using three checks: **external evidence, calculations, and internal consistency.**

These features improve the quality of audit documentation. The audit results will automatically be compiled into remarks and included in the export.

- **External evidence** — AI automatically links and validates figures with external evidence found in supporting documents. External evidence is re-checked when new versions of documents are uploaded.
- **Calculations** — AI detects calculations in audit documents and connects them to the figure with the result. Calculations can be added from both audit and supporting documents. These connections are automatically updated and re-validated when a new document version is uploaded.
- **Internal consistency** — If the same figure appears multiple times within the audit file, AI detects and connects them automatically. If one of the connected figures has external evidence, it will be applied to all of them.

## Add Remarks

For additional documentation, you can add your own remarks for each figure in the **remarks textbox** in the numbers section. Your remarks will appear in the final export document.

## Results

Results can be documented by clicking the **checkmark** or **cross** next to the figure. If the figure has connected external evidence, the result is already prefilled.

## Review

Within the review mode, each figure can be marked as reviewed.

## Flag Figures

Mark figures for later audit by clicking the **flag icon** next to a figure. The figure will be highlighted in **purple** and can be revisited and audited later.

## Delete Figures

To delete a figure, check the box next to it. An action bar will appear at the bottom of the screen. Click the **bin icon** to delete the figure.

<Note>
**Note:** It is possible to delete multiple figures at once by checking multiple boxes or by checking the box next to a page in the numbers section.
</Note>
