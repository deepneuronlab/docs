## 1. AI Identifies Internal Consistency

The **Internal Consistency** check identifies all figures that are linked to one specific audit figure, for example, figures that appear multiple times in the document or are connected through external evidence or calculations. These connected figures are grouped together under the **"Internal"** tab in the numbers section for cross-checking.

Once a connected number is reviewed, all its occurrences throughout the document are automatically marked as reviewed and do not require separate verification.

## 2. AI Suggests Internal Consistency

AI will provide suggestions for "Internal consistency" and indicate whether this link is **consistent** or **inconsistent** within the document. **"Internal"** suggestions are highlighted yellow and require approval by the auditor.

## 3. Add Internal Consistency

With the **"Connect Number"** feature, located in the bottom left corner of the "internal" box, additional numbers can be connected to "Internal Consistency" of an audit figure.

## 4. Delete Internal Consistency

To delete connected numbers, open the respective figure in **"Internal",** and click the **bin icon** next to it.
