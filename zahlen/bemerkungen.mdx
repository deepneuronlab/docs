## 1. Add Remarks

The **"Remarks"** feature serves as a form of reasoning for Calculations, Evidences, or Internal Consistency checks that have been assessed as consistent or inconsistent.

In ai/numbers, remarks prepared by AI are only visible in the exported file. The remarks only state the results that were confirmed by the user.

For additional documentation, remarks can be added to each figure in the **remarks textbox** within the numbers section. These user-created remarks will also be included in the numbers export file.

## 2. Remarks in the Export File

In the exported file, all figures that were listed in the numbers section will be marked **green** (if confirmed), **red** (if rejected) or blue (if the figure was not checked). Clicking on the marks next to the figure opens a pre-filled comment containing information about the figure checks (e.g. **"calculation valid"**) or the user-created remarks.

## 3. Delete Remarks

User-created Remarks can be deleted by removing the text from the Remarks text box in the numbers section. Once the text is cleared, the remark is removed automatically.
