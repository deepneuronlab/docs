## Upload a New Version of a Document

A new version of an audit document or a supporting document can be uploaded to the assignment via the **File Manager.**

By clicking **"Upload Documents"** in the top right corner of the File Manager, the user can choose to add **a new version of an audit document or a supporting document.** Next to all listed documents, the button **"Upload a new version"** allows adding a document directly from the computer.

Adding new document versions may result in lost figures in the numbers section. In this case, the corresponding figure in the numbers section will be struck through and display the option **"Reconnect."** By clicking **"Reconnect",** an updated figure can be added to the audit directly from the document.

The app automatically highlights differences between document versions. With the tab **"View Version Differences"** above the document view, you can enter the compare flow to review changes side by side.

When comparing versions, differences are classified as **editorial**, **substantive (expected)**, or **substantive (unexpected)**. You can filter annotations by classification to focus on the changes most relevant to your review. Clicking on highlighted text or figures navigates to the corresponding figure in the numbers section.

<Note>
The diff comparison may take a moment to process. A loading indicator is shown while the comparison is being prepared.
</Note>

This functionality ensures that all changes between document versions are clearly visible and can be reviewed.

<Warning>
**Note:** The new version upload must be confirmed by clicking **"Confirm"** in the bottom right corner of the window. Before confirming, it is still possible to delete the uploaded version. After confirmation, the newly added version cannot be deleted, and the numbers audit will automatically be updated and rechecked.
</Warning>
