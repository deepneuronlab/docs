DNL Audit is an AI-powered audit platform that automates routine validations so you can focus on the judgment calls that require human expertise. It combines structured digital checklists, automated figure verification, and intelligent account mapping in a single cloud-based workspace.

## Core Modules

DNL Audit consists of three core modules, each designed to handle a specific part of the audit process:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="ai/checklist" icon="list-check" href="/checklist/index.en">
    AI-powered digital checklists that auto-populate responses based on your audit documents. The AI suggests answers, links references, and adapts dynamically to company-specific criteria such as size, materiality, and legal form.
  </Card>
  <Card title="ai/numbers" icon="chart-bar" href="/numbers/index.en">
    Automated figure verification that validates calculations, checks internal and external consistency, and compares figures against supporting documents — surfacing mismatches and confirming matching figures across thousands of data points.
  </Card>
  <Card title="ai/accounts" icon="right-left" href="/account-mapping/index.en">
    Intelligent account mapping that reconciles financial accounts from your trial balance with taxonomy positions. The AI proposes mappings, validates them mathematically, and lets you review and adjust each mapping.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How a Typical Audit Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Client and Assignment">
    Set up your client and create a new assignment by selecting the product type (e.g., Notes, Management Report, Sustainability Report), choosing a checklist, and answering assignment criteria. The AI uses these criteria to tailor its analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Documents">
    Upload your audit documents and supporting documents via the File Manager. The AI automatically begins analyzing the documents — extracting figures, identifying references, and preparing checklist answers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Work Through the Checklist">
    Review AI-suggested answers in **ai/checklist**. Each answer links directly to references in your documents. Accept, adjust, or override suggestions, and add remarks for your audit documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the Figures">
    In **ai/numbers**, review the figures the AI has detected. Check external evidence from supporting documents, verify calculations, and confirm internal consistency across the document.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map Accounts (if applicable)">
    Use **ai/accounts** to reconcile trial balance accounts with taxonomy positions. Accept AI proposals, add target values for better accuracy, and resolve remaining open accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Export">
    A reviewer checks all prepared work in review mode. Once complete, export the results as PDF (with annotations), Word (with comments and proposed changes), or Excel (tabular figure overview).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Capabilities

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **AI-powered preparation** | The AI suggests answers, detects figures, links evidence, and proposes account mappings — automating routine validations. |
| **Document version tracking** | Upload new document versions at any time. The AI automatically detects changes, highlights differences, and re-validates affected items. |
| **Prepare and review workflow** | Built-in two-stage workflow with separate prepare and review modes, ensuring four-eyes oversight. |
| **Multi-language support** | Available in 8 languages: German, English, Swedish, Czech, Spanish, French, Italian, and Portuguese. |
| **Cloud collaboration** | Centralized workspace with role-based access, real-time updates, and multi-user support. |
| **Secure and compliant** | GDPR-compliant and ISO 27001-certified. All data hosted in Europe. |
