Open the login page: [**app.dnl.ai**](https://app.dnl.ai/) and choose your login method.

## Log in with your DNL account

Make sure your email address and password have been entered correctly. If you have forgotten your password, please contact us at [**support@dnl.ai**](mailto:support@dnl.ai).

## Log in with your Microsoft account credentials

If your email address has not been verified, please contact us at [**support@dnl.ai**](mailto:support@dnl.ai).
