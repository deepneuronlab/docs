## Provide Feedback Directly in the App

DNL Audit includes an integrated feedback system that allows you to send comments, bug reports, and improvement suggestions directly from the application.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Feedback Button">
    Click the **"Help / Feedback" button** in the application.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Feedback Type">
    Select whether you want to send a **bug report**, an **improvement suggestion**, or **general feedback**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Description">
    Describe your concern in detail. The more information you provide, the better we can help.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Screenshot">
    Optionally, you can create a **screenshot** that will be automatically attached to your feedback.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **"Send"**. Your feedback will be forwarded directly to the DNL team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Your feedback is very important to us! We actively use your input to continuously improve DNL Audit. Especially insights about interrelated checklist questions or new findings in the numbers audit help us optimize the AI models.
</Tip>
