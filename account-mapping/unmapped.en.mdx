## The Open Panel

The left panel shows all accounts that have not yet been assigned to a taxonomy position. Each account is displayed as a card with:

- **Account number** (e.g., `#1000`)
- **Account name**
- **Current year value** and **previous year value** (when available)
- **AI proposals** — ranked suggestions for where the account belongs

## Accept an AI Proposal

Each unmapped account may show up to 3 AI-ranked proposals in a dropdown. Proposals are marked with a yellow **"AI"** icon.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the proposal">
    The top-ranked proposal is shown by default. Click the dropdown to see alternative proposals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept">
    Click the **checkmark button** to accept the proposal. The account moves to the "Mapped" panel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Manually Map an Account

If no proposal fits, you can manually assign an account:

1. Click the **three-dot menu** (⋯) on the account card
2. Select **"Map"**
3. Choose the correct taxonomy position from the category picker
4. The account moves to the "Mapped" panel

## Bulk Actions

- **Accept all proposals** — Accept all AI proposals at once
- **Accept all selected proposals** — Accept proposals for selected accounts only

## Archive Accounts

Accounts that are not relevant can be archived:

1. Click the **three-dot menu** (⋯) on the account card
2. Select **"Archive"**
3. The account is hidden from the open list

Archived accounts can be restored by selecting **"Unarchive"** from the archived accounts section.

## Priority Accounts

Priority accounts are highlighted with special styling and should be mapped first. Use the **"Jump to priority account"** button at the bottom of the panel to navigate directly to the next unmapped priority.

The progress bar at the bottom shows: `<mapped count> / <total count>` priority accounts completed.
