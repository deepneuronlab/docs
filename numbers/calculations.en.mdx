## 1. AI Validated Calculations

The Calculations check in the numbers section displays all calculations detected in the document, alongside the original figures and results. AI automatically validates the mathematical consistency of each calculation and highlights the corresponding figures in green, both in the numbers section and within the document itself.

## 2. AI Proposal in Calculations

For some calculations AI will propose mathematical **consistency or inconsistency.** Those calculations will be marked yellow in the numbers section and require your review and confirmation.

If a calculation is flagged as inconsistent, for example due to a minor deviation, selecting **"Tolerate differences"** within the calculations allows the audit process to continue with the calculation confirmed and included.

## 3. Add Calculations

The **"Add Calculation"** feature allows additional calculations to be linked to an existing audit figure. Highlighting the calculation in the document and selecting **"Add Calculation"** in the appearing menu box adds it to the figure.

Alternatively, the **"Calculations"** button in the numbers section can be used. By selecting **"Add Calculation"** in the bottom right of the calculations box, relevant figures in the document can be highlighted and included.

Values from supporting documents can also be incorporated into the calculation for a figure from the main audit document if necessary.

## 4. Adjust Operations for Calculation

In the calculation section, calculations can be adjusted by selecting one of the four basic operations: Addition (+), Subtraction (–), Multiplication (×), or Division (÷).

Additionally, the AI detects percentage changes with the **Δ% operator**. It is possible to manually verify calculations and choose to display results as decimals or formatted percentages when comparing with expected values.

<Note>
**Note:** Only figures already present in the audit document can be used. Custom values, such as fixed factors (e.g., "2" or "100"), cannot be entered.
</Note>

## 5. Delete Calculations

To delete a linked calculation, open the respective number in the Calculations section and click the **bin icon** next to it.
