## 1. Expand All

The **"Expand All" button** in the action bar above the numbers section opens all pages with identified figures simultaneously. Next to it, the **"Collapse All" button** closes them again.

## 2. Document View Mode

Users can choose between the **"Dual View" or the "Single View" Mode** in the action bar above the numbers section. Switching between the two modes allows working with two documents side by side, facilitating comparisons and quick actions. For more focus, single view mode can be chosen.

## 3. Filter Figures

The **"filter" button** is located in the top-left area of the App, above the numbers section. Figures can be filtered based on different criteria, such as external evidence, calculations, or internal consistency. Figures can also be filtered through combined criteria.

<Note>
When a figure is added or edited while filters are active, the figure may be hidden in the numbers section due to the selected filter criteria.
</Note>

## 4. Flag Figures

Figures can be flagged for re-evaluation by clicking the **flag button** on the right side of the figures tab. The figure will then be highlighted in **purple**.

Flags can be removed by clicking the **"Remove Flag" button** again. All flags can be cleared at once using the flag button in the action bar via Multi Select.

<Note>
**Note:** Flagged figures cannot be reviewed. To set the review-checkmark, the figure must be unflagged and checked.
</Note>

## 5. Select All & Multi Select

Performing actions on multiple figures or pages simultaneously is possible using the **"Multi Select"** option. Multi Select can be accessed by checking the box next to the figures or pages. Once selected, an action bar appears at the bottom of the screen, offering a range of actions, including:

- flag figures
- add remarks
- confirm figures
- reject figures
- delete figures

To select all pages at once, the **"Select All"** button above the numbers section can be used to check all pages within that section.

## 6. Progress Bar

The **progress bar** next to the filter provides the total number of figures and displays how many figures fall into each of the defined categories. The **progress bar** shows the overall progress and indicates how many figures are:

- **prepared**
- **flagged by the user**
- **flagged by Notes Auditor**
- **reviewed**
- **not started**
