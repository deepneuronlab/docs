## 1. Audit Remarks

Audit remarks provide reasoning for the answer suggestion of every question. The preparer can delete (bin icon in the text field), or edit an existing AI generated remark, and also add reasoning, based on the selected answer of the preparer. Those remarks will be part of your final export document.

<Note>
**Note:** Rejecting an AI generated proposal, or manually changing the answer does not have an effect on the remarks. The preparer is obliged to change remarks manually and document the reasoning for the chosen answer.
</Note>

## 2. Internal Notes

Leave an **internal note** to exchange information, hints or questions for internal cooperators or team members. Those internal notes will **not** be displayed in any export document.
