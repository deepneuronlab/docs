## Create a New Assignment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Assignments Overview">
    Navigate to the desired client and open the **Assignments Overview**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Assignment">
    Click the **"Create Assignment"** button. A multi-step form will open.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Assignment Details">
    Select the **product type** (e.g., Notes, Management Report, Sustainability Report) and enter the required information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Checklist and Assignment Criteria">
    Select the appropriate **checklist** for your assignment. Available checklists depend on your organization group. Answer the **assignment criteria** questions relevant for AI-assisted preparation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Assignment">
    Review all inputs and confirm the assignment. Processing starts automatically — progress is shown in the Assignments Overview.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Certain assignment criteria are mutually exclusive. For example, a company cannot be classified as both a **financial institute** and an **insurance** company at the same time. If an invalid combination is selected, you will receive an error message.
</Warning>

<Note>
After creating the assignment, AI-powered processes are automatically started, including document analysis, number recognition, and checklist preparation. This may take a few minutes.
</Note>
