## Create a New Client

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Clients Overview">
    Navigate to the **Clients Overview** — this is displayed automatically after login.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Client">
    Click the **"Add Client"** button. Enter the **client name** and **client number**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Confirm your entry. The new client will appear in the Clients Overview.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Client names and numbers must be unique within your organization. If a client with the same number already exists, you will receive an error message.
</Note>
